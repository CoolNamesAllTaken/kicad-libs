PANTS FOR BIRDS LLC GENERAL PCB FABRICATION NOTES VERSION 20250730

THE FOLLOWING NOTES APPLY UNLESS OTHERWISE SPECIFIED

1. GENERAL STANDARDS
	A. FABRICATE PCB IN ACCORDANCE WITH THE CURRENT REVISION OF IPC-6012, CLASS 2, AND IPC-A-600.
	B. INSPECT PCBS IN ACCORDANCE WITH THE CURRENT REVISION OF IPC-A-600.
2. ALL DIMENSIONS IN MM.
3. STACKUP
	A. DEFAULT MATERIAL: FR4 TG 150C OR EQUIVALENT.
	B. THICKNESS OF INDIVIDUAL COPPER CLAD SHEETS SHALL BE AS DEFINED IN STACK-UP.
4. IMPEDANCE CONTROL
	A. ALL TOLERANCES +/- 10%.
	B. VENDOR MAY ADJUST DESIGN GEOMETRIES UP TO +/-20% TO ACHIEVE TARGET IMPEDANCE. ADJUSTMENTS BEYOND +/-20%
	   OF LINE WIDTH, SPACING, OR DIELECTRIC THICKNESS SHALL REQUIRE APPROVAL FROM PANTS FOR BIRDS LLC.
5. ETCH GEOMETRY
	A. MEASURE WIDTH FROM THE BASE OF THE METALIZATION.
	B. FINISHED LINE WIDTH AND TERMINAL AREA SHALL NOT DEVIATE FROM THE 1-TO-1 MASTER PATTERN IMAGE BY MORE THAN
	   +/- 25 UM OR 20%, WHICHEVER IS LESS.
6. HOLES
	A. PLATING IN HOLES SHALL BE CONTINUOUS ELECTROLYTIC COPPER WITH 25 UM MINIMUM BARREL WALL THICKNESS.
	B. HOLE SIZE MEASURED AFTER PLATING.
7. SURFACE FINISH
	A. ENIG PLATING AFTER SOLDERMASK PER CURRENT REVISION OF IPC-4552.
8. SOLDERMASK
	A. SOLDERMASK OVER BARE COPPER (SMOBC) ON PRIMARY AND SECONDARY SIDES USING SUPPLIED ARTWORK IN ACCORDANCE
	   WITH CURRENT REVISION OF IPC-SM-840 TYPE B.
	B. DEFAULT COLOR: BLACK.
	C. LIQUID PHOTO-IMAGEABLE (LPI) 1-2 UM THICKNESS.
	D. NO BLEED-OUT ALLOWED OVER EXPOSED PADS.
9. LEGEND (SILKSCREEN)
	A. LEGEND ON PRIMARY AND SECONDARY SIDE MUST BE PRINTED WITH WHITE NON CONDUCTIVE PERMANENT INK.
	B. CLIP LEGEND AWAY FROM EXPOSED METAL.
	C. VENDOR AND APPROVAL MARKINGS IN DEDICATED MARKING AREA ONLY.
10. FLATNESS
	A. BOW AND TWIST OF ASSEMBLY SUB-PANEL OR SINGULATED PCB SHALL NOT EXCEED 2.5% PER IPC-2422-2.
	B. TEST IN ACCORDANCE WITH THE CURRENT REVISION OF IPC-TM-650 2.4.22.
11. REMOVE ALL BURRS AND BREAK SHARP EDGES R0.1MM MIN.
12. NON-DESTRUCTIVE EVALUATION.
	A. ALL PCBS SHALL PASS 100% ELECTRICAL TEST USING THE SUPPLIED NETLIST IN ACCORDANCE WITH CURRENT REVISION OF
	   IPC-9252, CLASS 2.
	B. CERTIFICATE OF CONFORMANCE SHALL BE SUPPLIED WITH EACH SHIPMENT.
13. PANELS
	A. PANELIZATION DRAWINGS MUST BE APPROVED BY PANTS FOR BIRDS LLC BEFORE FABRICATION.
	B. PANELS WITH X-OUTS SHALL NOT BE USED FOR ASSEMBLY.
14. PACKAGING REQUIREMENTS
	A. PCBS SHALL BE PACKAGED IN VACUUM SEALED INNER CONTAINERS.
	B. OUTER CONTAINERS SHALL BE SUFFICIENT TO PREVENT DAMAGE DURING SHIPPING AND HANDLING.
